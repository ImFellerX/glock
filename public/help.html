Frequently Asked Questions

How do I deploy a new instance?

You can deploy a new instance from your dashboard. Simply click on "Deploy New Instance" and follow the steps to select your server specifications and start playing.

How do I add funds to my account?

You can add funds from the "Your Account" section in the navigation bar. Select "Add Credits" and follow the on-screen instructions to complete your payment securely.

What happens if my connection is unstable?

For the best experience, we recommend a stable internet connection with a minimum of 25 Mbps. If you experience issues, try switching to a wired connection or reducing the streaming quality in your settings.
